the easy and effective communication platform between people and robots

IoRT.io is an internet platform built to orchestrate, manage, and communicate physical and digital robots, physical objects, and processes to safely and intelligently interact with people and the environment.

Easy and fast communication with people.

The platform facilitates direct communication with the systems in an easy and intuitive way.

Execution of processes while monitoring the environment.

If a change occurs, they are able to recognize it and act appropriately autonomously, making optimal decisions.

Integration of email, spreadsheets, ERPs, CRMs and messaging directly with robotic systems.

Providing an agile way to introduce changes, order new processes or simply obtain feedback or information.

The IoRT.io platform enables the convergence of real, digital, virtual and cyber environments and the creation of combined intelligent environments.

IoRT.io is now being used by the Utobot and Green Pilgrim systems. It will soon be open to other companies and organizations that want an Internet of Robotic Things (IoRT) Platform.
